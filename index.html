My dearest Ashra,

When I look at these memories, I do not just see pictures. I see the way your presence changed my day. I see comfort. I see warmth. I see the kind of feeling that stays after the moment is gone. You have this beautiful way of making everything feel gentle and alive at the same time.

I love the happiness in your smile. I love the sweetness in your expressions. I love the quiet little moments where you are not even trying, yet you become the most beautiful part of everything around you. I love how naturally my heart moves toward you.
